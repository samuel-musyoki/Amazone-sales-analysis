# Amazone-sales-analysis

This project focused on analyzing Amazon sales performance using SQL and Power BI to extract meaningful business insights from transactional data.

The goal was to evaluate product category performance, state-level sales distribution, and overall profitability.

Tools & Technologies Used

SQL  Data cleaning, aggregation, and querying

Power BI  Interactive dashboard creation and visualization

Relational sales dataset for transaction data

 Project Objectives

Calculate total sales and total profit

Analyze sales performance by product category

Identify the highest and lowest performing products

Compare sales distribution across different states

Determine the best and worst performing regions

Key Insights

Product Category Performance

Highest performing product: Dell XPS 15

Total Sales: 24,502 million

Lowest performing product: iPhone 16

Total Sales: 7,853

This indicates strong demand for high-performance computing devices compared to mobile devices in this dataset.

Sales by State

Highest performing state: USA

Total Purchases: 15k

Lowest performing state: India

Total Purchases: 2,434

The data shows a significant sales concentration in the USA market compared to other regions.

 Overall Business Performance

Total sales were 102.33k

Total profit analysis was conducted to evaluate revenue efficiency

The dashboard provides clear insights into regional demand and product trends
